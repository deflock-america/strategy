## Request Pursuant to State Public Records Act

I request the following records related to Flock Safety ALPR cameras deployed in [CITY]:

### 1. All Flock Safety Queries & Search Results (2024-2025)
- Officer/user name who conducted search
- Timestamp of search
- License plate searched
- Search result (match/no match)
- Reason for search documented
- Any data accessed regarding the plate holder

### 2. Federal Agency Access Logs
- All federal agency access to Flock database (CBP, ICE, FBI, DEA, DHS, etc.)
- Agency name, officer name, timestamp, plates searched, reason
- Memoranda of Understanding (MOUs) between federal agencies and city

### 3. Hotlist Criteria & Sources
- What triggers a "hit" in Flock database?
- Are immigration lists included? Wanted persons? Protest participants?
- Source data for all hotlists
- Approval chain for hotlist additions

### 4. Camera Locations & Maintenance Records
- All camera installation locations (addresses, GPS coordinates)
- Installation dates
- Maintenance records
- Equipment specifications
- Upgrade/replacement history

### 5. Flock Safety Contracts
- Complete contract terms with Flock Safety
- Cost/annual budget
- Federal funding sources or grants
- Cost-sharing arrangements
- Data retention policies (how long retained?)
- Deletion/purge schedules

### 6. Security & Data Breaches
- Any data breaches or unauthorized access incidents
- Security audit reports
- Vulnerability assessments
- Hacking incidents or attempts

## Important Notes

**Washington Precedent:** Washington Superior Court (Nov 2025) ruled that Flock ALPR data IS public record, even when stored on private servers. Similar rulings likely in your state.

**If Denied:** File administrative appeal. Cite Washington precedent. Contact ACLU/EFF for litigation support.
